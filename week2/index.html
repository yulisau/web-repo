The Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Summary

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales. It's one of two breeds of cattle herding dogs known as the Welsh Corgi.

Appearance

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi has firm, medium sized, erect ears that taper slightly to a rounded point. Pembroke Welsh Corgis are shorter in length and have smaller ears than the Cardigan Welsh Corgi. Some Pembroke's are born with their tail naturally short but the majority often have their tails docked between 2-5 days old.

Temperament

Pembroke Welsh Corgis are great family dogs. They have a big desire to please their owners and follow them wherever they go. However, Pembroke's can be challenging to train due to their working background, stubborness and intelligence.
